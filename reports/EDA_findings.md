# Key EDA Findings

1. NAV values displayed a consistent upward trend from 2022–2026.
2. SBI Mutual Fund maintained the highest AUM across fund houses.
3. SIP inflows steadily increased during the analysis period.
4. December 2025 recorded the highest SIP inflow.
5. Equity-oriented categories attracted the largest inflows.
6. Investor participation increased significantly across all age groups.
7. T30 cities contributed a major share of investments.
8. Total folio count nearly doubled from 2022 to 2025.
9. Portfolio allocations were concentrated in a few major sectors.
10. Most mutual fund schemes exhibited strong positive correlations.
